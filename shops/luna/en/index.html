Important Information

To ensure a safe and relaxing experience, please take a moment to review the following:

  • If you experience any discomfort, pain, or changes in your physical condition during the treatment, please inform your therapist immediately.
  • Massage pressure can be adjusted according to your preference. Please feel free to let your therapist know at any time.
  • Receiving treatment after consuming alcohol may place additional stress on the body.
  • We may be unable to provide treatment to guests with a fever, contagious illness, or severe skin conditions.
  • If you are pregnant or may be pregnant, please inform our staff before your treatment.
  • Our treatments are intended for relaxation and wellness purposes only and are not a substitute for medical care or treatment.
  • Any inappropriate behavior, harassment, or requests for sexual services are strictly prohibited. If such behavior occurs, the treatment will be terminated immediately without refund.
  • Some guests may experience temporary soreness, fatigue, or mild discomfort after a massage. These reactions are generally temporary and vary from person to person.
